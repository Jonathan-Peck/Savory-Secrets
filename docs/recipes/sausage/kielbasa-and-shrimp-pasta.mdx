## Ingredients
- 1 lb raw large peeled and deviened shimp
- 1 pound kielbasa sausage sliced into rounds
- 3 cups spinach
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 cloves of garlic
- ¼ teaspoon cracked black pepper or a pinch of ground pepper
- 8 ounces penne pasta cooked and drained
- ½ cup heavy cream or half and half for a lighter option
- ½ cup grated parmesan cheese optional

## Instructions 

1. Bring water to boil for pasta. while waiting for water to boil proceed to step two. (or prepare Ingredients such as cutting Kielbasa). When water boils add pasta.
2. Drizzle a large, 2-inch deep skillet with olive oil.
3. Add sausage, and garlic to the pan.
4. Saute over medium-high heat for 3-4 minutes until kielbasa
starts to brown around the edges.
5. Add spinich and cook until juices release.
6. Add cream and stir 1-2 minutes til thickened. Then stir in pasta.
7. In a separate pan cook shrimp 2-3 minutes until shrimp curls in a perfect U and is a light pink.
8. Season with pepper, and parmesan and stir well.
9. Garnish with cracked black pepper and fresh herbs if desired and serve.
