Kielbasa and Shrimp Pasta

## Ingredients
- 1 lb raw large peeled and deviened shimp
- 1 pound kielbasa sausage sliced into rounds
- 3 cups spinach
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 cloves of garlic
- ¼ teaspoon cracked black pepper or a pinch of ground pepper
- 8 ounces penne pasta cooked and drained
- ½ cup heavy cream or half and half for a lighter option
- ½ cup grated parmesan cheese optional

## Instructions 

1. **Cook the Pasta:**  
Bring a large pot of water to a boil and cook the pasta according to package instructions.

2. **Prepare Ingredients:**  
Meanwhile, slice the kielbasa and prepare the other ingredients.

3. **Brown the Kielbasa:**  
Heat olive oil in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add the sliced kielbasa and garlic, sautéing for 3-4 minutes until the sausage starts to brown.

4. **Wilt the Spinach & Add Cream:**  
Add the spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted. Pour in the cream and stir for 1-2 minutes until slightly thickened.

5. **Combine Pasta:**  
Add the drained pasta and toss to combine.

6. **Cook the Shrimp:**  
In a separate pan, cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes until pink and curled.

7. **Finish & Serve:**  
Add the shrimp, black pepper, and parmesan to the pasta mixture. Stir well, garnish with extra pepper or herbs if desired, and serve.
