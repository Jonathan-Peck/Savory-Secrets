## Ingredients
- 1 lb groud beef
- 1 lb ground pork (sage sausage)
- 1 lb ground turkey
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon oregano
- 1 tablespoon complete seasoning
- 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (Itallian seasoned)
- 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
- 1/2 onion, diced
- 1 green pepper, diced
- 1 red pepper, diced
- 2 tablespoons red cooking wine
- 1 packet of meatloaf seasoning

## Instructions 
1. Preheat smoker to 250 degrees F.
2. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients and mix well.
3. Form into a loaf and place on a baking sheet or in a loaf pan.
4. Place in the smoker and cook for 2-3 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 160 degrees F.
