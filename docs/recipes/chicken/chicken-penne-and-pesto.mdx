Chicken Penne and Pesto

## Ingredients
- 1 (16 ounce) penne pasta
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 chicken breast cut into thin strips or small chunks
- 2 cloves of garlic
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 1/4 cup of heavy cream
- 1/4 cup pesto
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

## Instructions 

1. **Cook the Pasta**  
    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

2. **Prepare the Skillet**  
    Heat butter and olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

3. **Cook the Chicken**  
    Add chicken and garlic and sauté until chicken is almost cooked.

4. **Make the Sauce**  
    Reduce heat and stir in salt, pepper, cream, pesto, and parmesan cheese. Cook until chicken is no longer pink inside.

5. **Combine and Serve**
Stir in cooked pasta.
