## Ingredients
- 1 Chicken Breast cut into 1" chunks
- 8 ounces fettuccine noodles

### Seasoning for chicken
- 1 tablespoon Canola/Vegetable Oil (any oil works for cooking chicken)
- 1 teaspoon Onion Powder
- 1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
- 1 teaspoon Parsley
- 1 teaspoon Basil
- 1 teaspoon Oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon Thyme

### Seasoning for sauce
- 1/2 tablespoon Basil
- 1/2 tablespoon Parsley
- 1/2 tablespoon Oregano
- 1/2 tablespoon Ground Onion Powder
- 1/2 tablespoon Ground Garlic Powder
- 1/2 tablespoon Black Pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon Thyme 
- 1/4 teaspoon Herbs de Pronvince (optional)

## Instructions 
**Serves 2**

1. Bring water to a boil. While waiting on the water to boil prepare and cook chicken. Once water is boiling add pasta.
2. Prepare chicken by cutting into 1" chunks or however you desire. 
3. Add oil to pan and add chicken. Lightly season with Black Pepper, Ground Onion Powder, Ground Garlic Powder, Basil, Parsley, Oregano, Thyme.
4. Cook 6-8 minutes until chicken is browned and fully cooked.
5. prepare Homemade Alfredo sauce if not using a jar of Alfredo.
6. Drain noodles (remove chicken from heat if you are still waiting for pasta).
7. In a large Pot add noodles and chicken. 
8. Add in Alfedo sauce, Black Pepper, Ground Onion Powder, Ground Garlic Powder, Basil, Parsley, Oregano, Thyme, and Herbs De Province. 
9. Mix everything together and Serve warm.
