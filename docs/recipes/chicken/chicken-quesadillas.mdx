## Ingredients 
**Serves 2-3**
- 4-6 flour tortillas
- 2 cups Mexican cheese
- 1 chicken breast
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 large red bell pepper diced
- 1 Large yellow/orange bell pepper
- 1 yellow onion diced
- 1/2 packet taco seasoning

## Instructions 
1. **Prepapre chicken:** 
:::note[ways to prepare chicken]

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="short strips" label="short strips">
  **Cut into skinny short strips**  
  1. **Preheat skillet**  
  Preheat a skillet with oil on medium heat.  
  2. **Add chicken and seasoning**  
  Add in the chicken and taco seasoning.  
  3. **Cook chicken**  
  Cook till done.
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="whole" label="whole">
  **Cook chicken whole on skillet**  
  1. **Preheat skillet**  
  Preheat a skillet with oil on medium heat.  
  2. **Add chicken**  
  Add in the chicken.  
  3. **Cook chicken**  
  Cook until done.  
  4. **Shred chicken**  
  Put chicken in a bowl and shred with 2 forks.  
  5. **Season chicken**  
  Add taco seasoning.
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="rotisserie" label="rotisserie">**Use store bought rotisserie chicken**<br/>Shred and add taco seasoning.</TabItem>
</Tabs>

:::
2. **Cook vegetables:**  
On a skillet, cook bell pepper and onion until cooked through. Set aside.

3. **Assemble quesadilla:**  
On a grate, place tortilla. Cover with cheese, chicken, pepper and onion. Add another tortilla on top.
:::info[Note]

Spray top of quesadilla with pam for a crunchy top.

:::
4. Set airfryer to 7 minutes at 370 degrees. 
5. Serve while the Quesadillas are still warm!
