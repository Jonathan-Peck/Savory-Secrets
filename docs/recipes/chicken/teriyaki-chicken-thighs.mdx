## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons dry sherry
- 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 teaspoons ground ginger
- 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 2 tablespoons worchestershire sauce
- 2 lbs skinless chicken thighs
- 2 teaspoons sesame seeds optional
## Instructions 

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="Broiled" label="Broiled">
    1. **Make Marinade**  
    Combine the soy sauce, worchestershire sauce, sherry, brown sugar, rice vinegar, garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes and stir until the brown sugar dissolves.

    2. **Marinate Chicken**  
    Transfer to a resealable plastic bag and add chicken. Seal the bag and marinate the chicken in the refrigerator, turning once, for 1 hour. The chicken can be marinated for up to 4 hours.

    3. **Broil Chicken**  
    Heat the broiler to high. Arrange the chicken on a broiler pan skin side down and broil until brown and crispy, 8 to 10 minutes.

    4. **Flip and Finish**  
    Flip the chicken and broil until almost cooked through, about 8 minutes longer. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and cook until the seeds turn golden brown and the chicken is done, 1 to 2 minutes longer.
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="Smoked" label="Smoked">
    1. **Make Marinade**  
    Combine the soy sauce, worchestershire sauce, sherry, brown sugar, rice vinegar, garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes and stir until the brown sugar dissolves.

    2. **Marinate Chicken**  
    Transfer to a resealable plastic bag and add chicken. Seal the bag and marinate the chicken in the refrigerator, turning once, for 1 hour. The chicken can be marinated for up to 4 hours.

    3. **Smoke Chicken**  
    Prepare smoker and cook chicken until internal temperature reaches 165ºF.
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
