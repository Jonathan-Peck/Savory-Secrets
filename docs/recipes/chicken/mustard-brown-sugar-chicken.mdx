Mustard Brown Sugar Chicken

## Ingredients
- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 3 cloves of Garlic, minced
- 1/3 cup light brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons mustard (yellow or brown/whole grain)
- 1 tablespoons rosemary
- 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper

## Instructions 


1. **Prepare the Chicken**  
Season chicken on both sides with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken and cook until golden and cooked through, about 8 minutes per side. Remove from skillet.

:::info[Note]
cutting chicken into cutlets can be easier to cook.
:::

2. **Make the Garlic Broth**  
Add butter to skillet then add chicken broth and garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.

3. **Create the Sauce and Finish**  
Add brown sugar, mustard, rosemary, and red pepper flakes. Stir until brown sugar is melted and a sauce forms. Add back chicken and spoon sauce over chicken.

:::info[Note]

serve with vegetables or beef/chicken rice-A-roni

:::
