## Ingredients
- 1 lb chicken cutlets
- 1/4 teaspoon salt, divied
- 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper, divided
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 cup chopped baby spinach
- 1/2 cup finely chopped red onion
- 1/3 cup roasted red peppers, thinly sliced
- 1/3 cup sun-dried tomato halves, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup dry white wine
- 3/4 cup sour cream
## Instructions 
1. Sprinkle chicken with 1/8 teaspoon each salt and pepper. 
2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. add chicken and cook, turning once, until cooked through, 6 to 8 minutes. Transfer to a plate.
4. Add spinach, onion, roasted red peppers and sun-dried tomatoes to the pan. Cook, stirring, for 1 minute. 
5. Increase heat to high and add wine Cook, scrapping up any browned bits, until the liquid is mostly evaporated, about 2 minutes. 
6. Reduce heat to medium and stir sour cream, any accumulated chicken jucies and the remaining 1/8 teaspoon each salt and pepper; simmer, stirring, for 2 minutes. 
7. Return the chicken to the pan and turn to coat.
8. Serve the chicken topped with the sauce.
