Kielbasa and Shrimp Pasta

## Ingredients
- 1 lb raw large peeled and deviened shimp
- 1 pound kielbasa sausage sliced into rounds
- 3 cups spinach
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 cloves of garlic
- ¼ teaspoon cracked black pepper or a pinch of ground pepper
- 8 ounces penne pasta cooked and drained
- ½ cup heavy cream or half and half for a lighter option
- ½ cup grated parmesan cheese optional

## Instructions 

1. **Boil Pasta**  
Bring water to boil for pasta. While waiting for water to boil, proceed to step two (or prepare ingredients such as cutting kielbasa). When water boils, add pasta.

2. **Heat Oil**  
Drizzle a large, 2-inch deep skillet with olive oil.

3. **Add Sausage and Garlic**  
Add sausage and garlic to the pan.

4. **Sauté**  
Sauté over medium-high heat for 3-4 minutes until kielbasa starts to brown around the edges.

5. **Add Spinach**  
Add spinach and cook until juices release.

6. **Add Cream and Pasta**  
Add cream and stir 1-2 minutes until thickened. Then stir in pasta.

7. **Cook Shrimp**  
In a separate pan, cook shrimp 2-3 minutes until shrimp curls in a perfect U and is a light pink.

8. **Season**  
Season with pepper and parmesan and stir well.

9. **Garnish and Serve**  
Garnish with cracked black pepper and fresh herbs if desired and serve.
