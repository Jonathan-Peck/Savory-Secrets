## Ingredients 
**Serves 2**
- 4-6 flour tortillas
- 2 cups Mexican cheese
- 1 chicken breast
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 large red bell pepper diced
- 1 Large yellow/Ooange bell pepper
- 1 yellow onion diced
- 1/2 packet taco seasoning

## Instructions 
1. Prepapre chicken: 
:::note[ways to prepare chicken]

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="short strips" label="short strips"> **cut into skinny short strips**<br/> Preheat a skillet with oil on medium heat. Add in the chicken and taco seasoning. cook till done.</TabItem>
  <TabItem value="whole" label="whole">**cook chicken whole on skillet**<br/>Preheat a skillet with oil on medium heat. Add in the chicken. cook for till done. Put chicken in bowl and shred with 2 forks. Add taco seasoning.</TabItem>
  <TabItem value="rotisserie" label="rotisserie">**Use store bought rotisserie chicken**<br/>shred and add taco seasoning.</TabItem>
</Tabs>

:::
2. On a skillet, cook bell pepper and onion until cooked through. Set aside.
3. On a grate, place tortilla. cover with cheese, chicken, pepper and onion. Add another tortilla on top.
:::info[Note]

Spray top of quesadilla with pam for a crunchy top.

:::
4. Set airfryer to 7 minutes at 370 degrees. 
5. Serve while the Quesadillas are still warm!
