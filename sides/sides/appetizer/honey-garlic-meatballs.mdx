## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup brown Sugar
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1/2 ketchup
- 2 tablespoons low lodium soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon worchestershire sauce
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 28 ounces (1lb 12 oz) frozen fully cooked meatballs

## Instructions 
<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="Slow Cooker" label="slow cooker">
    1. a medium bowl, whisk together the brown sugar, honey, ketchup, soy sauce, worchestershire, 
    and garlic.<br/>
    2. Place frozen meatballs in a 4-quart slow cooker and pour sauce over them. Stir to coat. Cook on low for 4 hours, 
    stirring occasinally.<br/>
    3. Serve as an appetizer, or over rice or noodles as a main dish.
  <TabItem value="Instant Pot" label="instant pot">
    1. Turn the instant pot on saute mode and combine brown sugar, honey, ketchup, soy sauce, worchestershire, and garlic. 
    Stir until somewhate thick and bubbly (it only takes a few minutes).<br/>
    2. Turn off saute mode and add the frozen meatballs. Stir to combine the meatballs with the sauce.<br/>
    3. Place the lid on the instant pot and set it to manual high pressure for 3 minutes.<br/>
    4. Once the cooking is done, quick release the pressure, open the lid, stir a little more and enjoy over rice!</TabItem>
  <TabItem value="Oven" label="oven">
    1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (or according to meatball package instructions).<br/>
    2. Place frozen meatballs in a single layer in a 9x13 pan. Bake for 14-16 minutes (or according to package instructions).<br/>
    3. Whisk together the brown sugar, honey, ketchup, soy sauce, worchestershire, and garlic in a suacepan over medium heat, until simmering and slightly thickened, about 5 minutes.<br/>
    4. Pour sauce over warm meatballs, stir to coat, and serve.</TabItem>
</Tabs>
