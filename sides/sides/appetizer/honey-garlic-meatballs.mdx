## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup brown Sugar
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1/2 ketchup
- 2 tablespoons low lodium soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon worchestershire sauce
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 28 ounces (1lb 12 oz) frozen fully cooked meatballs

## Instructions 
<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="slow cooker" label="Slow Cooker">
    **Slow Cooker Instructions**
    <br/>
    **Slow Cooker Instructions**
    <br/>
    1. **Make the Sauce**  
    In a medium bowl, whisk together the brown sugar, honey, ketchup, soy sauce, worchestershire, and garlic.
    <br/>
    2. **Add Meatballs**  
    Place frozen meatballs in a 4-quart slow cooker and pour sauce over them. Stir to coat.
    <br/>
    3. **Cook**  
    Cook on low for 4 hours, stirring occasionally.
    <br/>
    4. **Serve**  
    Serve as an appetizer, or over rice or noodles as a main dish.
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="instant pot" label="Instant Pot">
    **Instant Pot Instructions**
    <br/>
    1. **Make the Sauce**  
    Turn the Instant Pot on saute mode and combine brown sugar, honey, ketchup, soy sauce, worchestershire, and garlic. Stir until somewhat thick and bubbly (it only takes a few minutes).
    <br/>
    2. **Add Meatballs**  
    Turn off saute mode and add the frozen meatballs. Stir to combine the meatballs with the sauce.
    <br/>
    3. **Pressure Cook**  
    Place the lid on the Instant Pot and set it to manual high pressure for 3 minutes.
    <br/>
    4. **Finish & Serve**  
    Once the cooking is done, quick release the pressure, open the lid, stir a little more and enjoy over rice!
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="oven" label="Oven">
    **Oven Instructions**
    <br/>
    1. **Preheat Oven**  
    Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (or according to meatball package instructions).
    <br/>
    2. **Bake Meatballs**  
    Place frozen meatballs in a single layer in a 9x13 pan. Bake for 14-16 minutes (or according to package instructions).
    <br/>
    3. **Make the Sauce**  
    Whisk together the brown sugar, honey, ketchup, soy sauce, worchestershire, and garlic in a saucepan over medium heat, until simmering and slightly thickened, about 5 minutes.
    <br/>
    4. **Combine & Serve**  
    Pour sauce over warm meatballs, stir to coat, and serve.
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
