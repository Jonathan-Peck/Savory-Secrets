## Ingredients
- 1 bag stuffing - Pepperidge Farm or similar
- 1 lb ground round sirloin burger
- Turkey guts (neck, gizzards)
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup celery, diced
- 1/2 cup onion, diced
- 2 cans chicken broth
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon thyme
- 1 tablespoon marjoram
- salt and pepper to taste

:::info

experimental seasoning amounts need refining

:::

## Instructions 

1. **Prepare the Rue**  
   In a 2qt pot, fill 3/4 full of water, add onion, salt and pepper, turkey guts, thyme, and marjoram. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 40 minutes.

2. **Cook the Ground Beef**
   In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground round sirloin until browned. Drain any excess fat and set aside.

3. **Prepare stuffing**
    Add stuffing mix, clerey, onion, egg, cooked ground beef, and butter to a large bowl. Mix well. add small amounts of chicken broth until the stuffing is moist but not soggy.
4. **Combine and Cook**
   Add the stuffing mixture to the pot with the turkey guts and broth. Stir well to combine. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and cover with foil. Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for about 30-40 minutes, or until heated through and the flavors meld together. Uncover, raise temperature to 400°F (200°C) and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the top is golden brown.
