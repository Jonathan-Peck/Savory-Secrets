## Ingredients
- 2 canned sweet potatoes, drained and mashed
- 1/3 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon cloves
- 2 eggs
- 8 tablespoons butter, melted
- 16 ounces mini marshmallows

## Instructions 
1. **Preheat Oven**  
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

2. **Mix Ingredients**  
In a large bowl, mix together sweet potatoes, brown sugar, salt, cinnamon, cloves, eggs, and melted butter until well combined.

3. **Layer Mixture and Marshmallows**  
Pour half mixture into a baking dish, top with a layer of marshmallows, then add remaining mixture.

4. **Bake First Time**  
Bake for 30 minutes.

5. **Add Marshmallow Topping**  
Remove from oven and top with mini marshmallows.

6. **Bake Until Golden**  
Return to oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until marshmallows are golden brown and melted.

7. **Serve**  
Serve warm.
