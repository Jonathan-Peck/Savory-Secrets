## Ingredients
- Libbys’ Pumpkin Pie Can 20oz (2 pies)
- 2 9" deep dish frozen pie crusts
- 2 Evaporated Milk 12oz
- 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
- 3/4 cup White Sugar
- 2 tsp Cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon Cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon Ginger
- 1/8 teaspoon Black Pepper
- 4 Eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt 


Oven to 425 for 15 minutes then, lower to 350 for 45 minutes testing 1 hour at 350
## Instructions 
1. preheat oven to 425
2. mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl and Libby's pumpkin, add in eggs and finally add in Evaporated milk. 
3. pour mixture into pie crusts and with a spoon spread some of the mixture onto the pie crusts for extra flavor. 
4. cook pies for 15 minutes at 425 then lower to 350 for 45 minutes.
