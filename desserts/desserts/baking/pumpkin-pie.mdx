## Ingredients
- Libbys’ Pumpkin Pie Can 20oz (2 pies)
- 2 9" deep dish frozen pie crusts
- 2 Evaporated Milk 12oz
- 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
- 3/4 cup White Sugar
- 2 tsp Cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon Cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon Ginger
- 1/8 teaspoon Black Pepper
- 4 Eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt 


Oven to 425 for 15 minutes then, lower to 350 for 45 minutes testing 1 hour at 350
## Instructions 
1. **Preheat the Oven**  
Preheat oven to 425°F.

2. **Prepare the Filling**  
Mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl. Add Libby's pumpkin, then eggs, and finally evaporated milk.

3. **Fill the Pie Crusts**  
Pour mixture into pie crusts. With a spoon, spread some of the mixture onto the pie crusts for extra flavor.

4. **Bake the Pies**  
Bake pies for 15 minutes at 425°F, then lower to 350°F and bake for 45 minutes.
